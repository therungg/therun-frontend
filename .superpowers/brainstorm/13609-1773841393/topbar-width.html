A. Full-Width Edge-to-Edge

Bar spans the entire viewport. Content below is contained. The bar becomes a permanent horizon line — wide, authoritative. Standard for most major sites (GitHub, lichess, YouTube).

B. Floating Contained

Bar floats inside the page with rounded corners and a gap from edges. Feels like a glass island. More unique and premium, but loses the anchoring effect. Arc browser / macOS vibes.
